The International Conference for Upcoming Engineers (ICUE) was initiated by members of Ryerson University in 2001 to explore different options after graduation and to network with successful professionals in various engineering fields. Over the years, the conference has explored various hot topics of engineering including Design, Entrepreneurship, etc. To benefit upcoming engineers from a wide range of locations, the conference has been hosted by different universities each year. IEEE University of Toronto Student Branch has held ICUE three times - 2013, 2010 and 2008.
